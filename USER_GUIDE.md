# User Guide - Follow-Up Tracker

Welcome to the Follow-Up Tracker! This guide will help you understand how to navigate the platform and manage your tasks effectively.

## 1. Getting Started

### Roles & Access
Your experience on the platform depends on your assigned role:
- **Founders**: Can manage the entire organization and see all tasks.
- **Managers**: Can manage their department and view their team's tasks.
- **Employees**: Can track their own work and log tasks assigned to them orally.

### Profile Setup
Upon first login (especially via Google), ensure you complete your profile by selecting your **Role** and **Department**. You can update your Name and WhatsApp number at any time in the **Profile** section.

## 2. The Dashboard

The Dashboard is your command center. It features:
- **Analytics Chart**: A visual breakdown of your task statuses and deadlines.
- **Filter Chips**: Quickly toggle between "All", "In Progress", "Blocked", and "Completed" tasks.
- **Search**: Find tasks by title or assignee.

## 3. Task Lifecycle

### Creating a Task
Click **+ New Task** to create a task.
- **Assign to others**: Founders and Managers can assign tasks to team members.
- **Self-logging**: Employees can use this to record tasks they are currently working on.

### Accepting Tasks
When a task is assigned to you, it starts in the **Pending Acceptance** state. You must **Accept** or **Reject** the task. If you reject it, you must provide a reason.

### Updating Status
Keep your team informed by updating the task status:
- **Not Started**: Task is in the queue.
- **In Progress**: You are actively working on it.
- **Blocked**: You are stuck and need help (this triggers a notification to the creator).
- **Completed**: Work is finished (this triggers a notification to the creator).

## 4. Communication & History

### Comments
Use the comment section inside any task (click on the task card to open) to provide updates, ask questions, or share files/links.

### Task History
Finished or rejected tasks are automatically moved to the **Task History** section. This keeps your main dashboard clean and focused on active work.

## 5. Management (Founder & Managers Only)

### Organization Settings
Founders can access the **Settings** menu to:
- **Manage Departments**: Add or remove departments in the organization.
- **Team Manager**: View all members, change their roles, or update their department assignments.

## 6. Notifications
The platform automatically sends alerts via **WhatsApp and Email** for:
- New tasks assigned to you.
- Tasks that have been marked as Blocked or Completed.
- Approaching deadlines (24-hour and 1-hour reminders).
